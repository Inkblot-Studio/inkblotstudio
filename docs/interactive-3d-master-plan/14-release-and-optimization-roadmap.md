# Phased Release and Optimization Roadmap

## Release Strategy

Launch progressively so authority and conversion are live early, while richer interaction layers are added with controlled risk.

## Phase A - Authority + Conversion Core

### Objective

Establish trust, clear positioning, and reliable strategy-call conversion.

### Scope

- Homepage with primary narrative and offer ladder.
- Solutions and How We Work pages.
- Strategy Call flow with qualification form and booking.
- Credibility baseline assets (methodology, architecture, anonymized outcomes).

### Success Signals

- Strategy call bookings reaching baseline target.
- Qualification completion rate above threshold.
- Stable web vitals under production traffic.

## Phase B - Premium Interactive Expansion

### Objective

Add distinctive luxury interaction layer that increases engagement without harming conversion speed.

### Scope

- Tiered 3D hero implementation (A/B/C capability modes).
- Scroll-linked narrative modules.
- Enhanced trust/proof interactions.

### Success Signals

- Interaction engagement lift without drop in CTA conversion.
- No significant performance regression in mid-capability devices.
- Improved branded differentiation feedback from prospects.

## Phase C - Productized Proof + Personalization

### Objective

Strengthen market authority and lead quality with product trajectory and segment-aware experiences.

### Scope

- Citron ecosystem proof narratives and operator methodology deepening.
- Segment-specific messaging variants and CTA pre-qualification logic improvements.
- Expanded proof library with additional anonymized transformation assets.

### Success Signals

- Higher lead quality and stronger discovery-call fit.
- Improved conversion from strategy call to paid engagement.
- Shorter sales-cycle progression due to better pre-qualification.

## Optimization Loop (Per Phase)

1. Collect conversion and behavior data.
2. Diagnose friction by segment and device tier.
3. Prioritize highest-impact content/UX/performance changes.
4. Run controlled iteration release.
5. Validate against KPI movement and repeat.

## KPI Ownership Cadence

- Weekly: funnel metrics, conversion friction, qualitative sales feedback.
- Bi-weekly: interaction and performance trend review.
- Monthly: lead-quality, close-rate, and messaging effectiveness recalibration.

## Risk Controls

- Roll back high-cost interaction features if conversion or performance degrades.
- Freeze new visual experimentation when CTA funnel drops below threshold.
- Require claim validation before publishing new proof assets.

## Decision Gates

- **Gate A to B:** conversion baseline and performance guardrails stable.
- **Gate B to C:** interaction layer proves net positive impact on engagement and lead quality.
- **Gate C scale-up:** sufficient proof assets and repeatable sales outcomes established.

## Decision / Rationale / Implication

- **Decision:** run a controlled phased rollout instead of one full-feature launch.
- **Rationale:** this maximizes speed to revenue while protecting quality and brand credibility.
- **Implication:** roadmap execution must prioritize conversion integrity first, then experiential depth.
