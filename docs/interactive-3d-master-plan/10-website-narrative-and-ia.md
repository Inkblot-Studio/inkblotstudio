# Website Narrative and Information Architecture

## Conversion Objective

Drive qualified visitors to `Book strategy call` with high intent and clear project context.

## Journey Narrative Framework

### Step 1: Differentiate

- Communicate premium capability and AI systems authority in the first screen.
- Promise tangible operational acceleration with privacy-first control.

### Step 2: Reframe the Problem

- Show why dashboards and fragmented tools produce stress and latency.
- Introduce chat-first, agentic workflows as a simpler decision interface.

### Step 3: Present the Offer Ladder

- Clarify path: Audit -> Build -> Managed Optimization.
- Emphasize that each stage reduces risk and increases measurable outcomes.

### Step 4: Build Trust

- Present proof substitutes while public case studies are limited.
- Show technical governance, delivery methodology, and anonymized transformations.

### Step 5: Capture Intent

- Lead to a single primary conversion endpoint: strategy call booking.
- Use short pre-qualification prompts to improve lead quality.

## Sitemap (Phase A Core)

- Home
- Solutions (by pain pattern and segment)
- How We Work (delivery model and governance)
- Proof (methodology, anonymized outcomes, product vision evidence)
- About Inkblot (team and operating philosophy)
- Strategy Call (booking and qualification)

## Home Page Module Order

1. Hero (luxury innovation + clear business promise)
2. Problem Reframe (why current workflows fail)
3. Offer Ladder (audit/build/retainer)
4. Segment Lens (who this is for)
5. Trust Architecture (method, governance, outcome evidence)
6. Product Trajectory (Citron vision as strategic moat)
7. Final CTA (book strategy call)

## CTA Architecture

### Primary CTA

- Label: `Book strategy call`
- Placement: header, hero, mid-page after offer ladder, final section, sticky mobile action.

### Supporting CTAs

- `Start with AI Workflow Audit`
- `See how delivery works`
- `Review operating methodology`

Supporting CTAs should always route back toward strategy call intent.

## Pre-Qualification Flow (Before Calendar)

- Company type / industry
- Main operational bottleneck
- Desired timeline
- Preferred engagement model (audit / build / build+retainer)
- Team size and implementation owner

This prevents low-fit bookings and improves discovery call quality.

## Content Hierarchy Rules

- Business outcome statements before feature language.
- One core idea per section with one action path.
- Keep premium visual treatment while preserving scanning clarity.
- Avoid product over-claiming while assets are still in development.

## Analytics Mapping

- `hero_primary_cta_click`
- `offer_ladder_expand`
- `proof_module_view`
- `qualification_start`
- `qualification_complete`
- `calendar_booking_complete`

## Decision / Rationale / Implication

- **Decision:** enforce single dominant conversion path with supporting trust-building branches.
- **Rationale:** focused conversion architecture outperforms multi-goal navigation for early-stage positioning.
- **Implication:** page copy, interaction rhythm, and module ordering should all serve strategy-call conversion momentum.
